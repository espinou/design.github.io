Accord Mobile Ticket App

Accord is an application focused on the sale and resale of concert tickets while also keeping ticket scalpers in mind by adding a ticket limit when buying and a limit to the price you can sell tickets to depepending on the original price of the ticket and the demand of the concert.

The application also shows the amount of tickets left prior to the concert so that people who are contemplating on buying tickets can know if they should wait or not since sometimes money is tight.
